tunnel.pyjam.as

SSL-terminated, ephemeral HTTP tunnels to a machine on your local network
without any custom software on your machine thanks to Wireguard.

To start a tunnel for your local service on port 8080

$ curl https://tunnel.publickey.sh/8080 > tunnel.conf && wg-quick up ./tunnel.conf

To stop your tunnel

$ wg-quick down ./tunnel.conf
